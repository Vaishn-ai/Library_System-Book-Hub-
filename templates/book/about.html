📖 About

Welcome to vi, BookHub, your vibrant, all-access reading playground where independent storytellers and classic authors come together with curious readers from every walk of life. We’ve built a warm, inclusive community that sparks creativity, celebrates every genre, and makes you feel right at home—whether you’re discovering hidden gems or rediscovering time-honored favorites.

✅

We’re transforming Books into a vibrant home for readers and writers to create, connect, and share—right on our platform. Soon, you’ll be able to read and write stories in-app, join writing circles to swap feedback and spark fresh ideas, and showcase your work to a community that’s always hungry for new voices. Whether you’re drafting your next chapter or diving into someone else’s tale, you’ll find everything you need to fuel your passion without ever leaving the site.

🎯 Purpose

For authors, we’ve streamlined the journey from manuscript to masterpiece: submit your story for a quick screening, get the green light to begin professional editing and cover design, and then publish with your final sign-off. Once your book is live, you’ll earn at least $0.001 per page every time a reader spends a credit to add it to their library.

🚀 Key Features

  • Different types of books: academic, novels, journals, magazines, etc.
  • Users can register as members
  • Members get access to borrow books
  • Issue books to users
  • Users can reserve a book if it is already issued
  • Daily/monthly report
